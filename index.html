Frequently Asked Questions

Mission

Kagio United FC aims to provide a platform for local talent to showcase their skills and ultimately reach the Kenya Premier League (KPL).

How can I support the team?

You can support the team by attending matches, following us on social media, and contributing to our fundraising efforts.

About Us

Kagio United FC was established in 2008 by passionate football enthusiasts in Kagio, a town located in Kenya’s Central Province.


Rise Through the Ranks

The team’s inception aimed to provide a platform for local talent to showcase their skills and compete at various levels. The club began its journey in grassroots football, participating in local tournaments and leagues.
